Colorado Avalanche 2021-2022 Regular Season

In 2021-2022, the Colorado Avalanche won the Stanley Cup after finishing second in points in the regular season. One of their main successes was their offensive prowess, led by superstar Nathan MacKinnon. In this article, I will present context on the Avalanche's 2021-2022 season and offensive statistics.
